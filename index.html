Pattern Generator

P5.js project where the task is to create a generator that produces some type of pattern. This generator was made as an exploration of the visual potential for the inkblot portion of the Rorschach test. The generated patterns focus on the elements of symmetry, form, and scale. Color may also be considered for the generated image. The generator creates a randomized number of shapes, which are mirrored on the other half of the canvas.
